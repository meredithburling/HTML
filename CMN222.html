ABOUT US



WELCOME

Kill Them With Colour is a contemporary art gallery located on 294 Queen Street West, Toronto. We embrace creativity and imagination of all kinds. We are a new company with the goal of seeking out new talent. Not only do we display artwork for the Toronto pubic, we also host events to unify the art community. We welcome new artists of any age who would like to share their art with the world. Art enthusiasts, or anyone passing by our gallery are also welcome to come in for a fee of $15. We hope you visit soon. Stay creative.
